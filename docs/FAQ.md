# Copa Mundial — FAQ

## The basics

### What is Copa Mundial?

A **free skill-based prediction game** for World Cup 2026. Reply on **X** with your score before kickoff, earn **points**, and climb the **leaderboard**. Each day at **10:00 UTC** the **top 20** on the cumulative board can win **USDC on Solana**.

### Is this gambling?

Skill-based predictions on public match results — not a sportsbook. Check your local laws. Not affiliated with FIFA or any federation. See [disclaimer](https://copamundial.app/disclaimer).

### Do I need a wallet to play?

**No.** You only need **X** to predict and earn points. Connect a **Solana wallet** (Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack) only if you finish in the top 20 and want to **claim USDC**.

---

## How to play

1. Follow **[@copamundialapp](https://x.com/copamundialapp)** on X.
2. Reply on the **match thread** with both teams and a score before kickoff — e.g. `Brazil 2-1 Morocco`.
3. Your **first valid reply** before kickoff is the one that counts.
4. After full time, points update automatically (usually within ~15 minutes).
5. Points from **every match** add to your **season total** on the leaderboard.

---

## Scoring

| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| **Exact score** | **5** |
| **Correct result** (right winner or draw, wrong score) | **3** |
| **Wrong result** | **1** |

When you get the **result right**, your base is multiplied by the **market odds locked before kickoff** (TxLINE 1X2) — up to **×3** when you back the underdog.

---

## Leaderboard & prizes

- **One rank per X account.** Total points = sum of all scored matches.
- **No daily or 3-day reset** — the board is cumulative across the tournament.
- **Daily snapshot at 10:00 UTC** locks the top 20 for that day’s USDC payout.
- **Tier split:** ranks 1–3 ~9.3% each · 4–10 ~5.6% each · 11–20 ~3.3% each (10 : 6 : 3.5 ratio, 100% of pool).

Pool size depends on what is funded in the Solana rewards vault — not a fixed guaranteed amount.

---

## Settlement

We use the score after **90 minutes + injury time** only. **Extra time and penalties do not** change your points.

Live scores and settlement use **TxLINE** scout-verified data.

---

## Wallets & claims

1. Sign in at **copamundial.app** with the same X account that earned points.
2. Open the **Wallet** tab → connect **Phantom**, **Solflare**, or **Backpack**.
3. After the **10:00 UTC** snapshot, top-20 winners can **Collect** USDC.

You can switch wallets before you claim. Prizes already sent stay on the old address.
